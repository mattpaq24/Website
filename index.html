Half-and-half

A collaborative origami experience

About

How it works

You will need:

• A partner

• Origami paper

Directions

· Instructions to fold an origami model are split into two sets: the odd-numbered instructions, and the even-numbered instructions.

· Choose one of these sets; make sure your partner selects the opposite set.

· At each step, instructions are only visible to one partner; the partner with the odd-numbered set can only see the instructions for odd-numbered steps, and vice versa.

· Partners take turns teaching the instructions to each other until the model is complete.

Background

I pitched Half-and-half as a student project in the summer of 2020 - I was prompted to create a product to help individuals socialize in a positive manner in the wake of COVID. Drawing from my own positive tutoring experiences, I wanted to create a product where participants could communicate in a collaborative manner to achieve a common goal.
